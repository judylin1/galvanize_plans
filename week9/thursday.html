  1. Build your own styleguide from the ground up using the same process described below

Giving Feedback

ASK: Actionable, specific, kind

Bad: "This looks terrible. I don't like it." This feedback ins't actionable, specific, or kind.

Good: "The colors you use are a bit distracting and it's hard for me to read the text with that background."

When someone shows you a design, ask yourself these questions:

  • As a first time user, is the purpose of this interface obvious right away?
  • As the user, is there anything distracting me from what I want to accomplish?
  • As the user, am I confident the app will do what I want it to do?
  • Do all of the UI elements appear to belong together? Are they cohesive?
